Nicholas Nooney's Spatial Research

Thank you for participating in my research project! This site will gather information about your perceptions of spatial ability and your performance on some spatial tasks. Before you submit your information, you will have the opportunity to review it.

Please read all instructions carefully before proceeding. The entire survey and tests will take approximately 30 minutes to complete. You will be able to see your progress via a progress bar at the top of the survey.

Tasks

You will be tested on four different tasks which will measure separate aspects of spatial ability. The four tests are:

  1. Mental Rotation Task
  2. Placing Buildings on a Map
  3. Tracing Routes from Stories
  4. Identifying Locations in Pictures
